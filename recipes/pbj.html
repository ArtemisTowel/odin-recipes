PBJ

Description

Once upon a time, George Washington Carver invented peanut butter. Somewhere between then and now we got PBJ's and the world is better for it.

    Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly of choice
  • Bread
  • Glass of milk

    Steps

  1. Open a loaf of bread and touch all the slices to grab yours.
  2. "Accidentally" lose the twist tie under a kitchen appliance.
  3. Spread peanut butter on one side of one of your pieces of bread
  4. Spread jelly on one side of the other piece of bread.
  5. Combine the sandwhich with the condemented sides in the middle.
  6. Pour an ice cold glass of milk to wash it all down.
