Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe would make any grandmother proud. One bite of this dish and your family will have you on the holiday hosting roster. The key to this recipe is the fresh shredded cheese and homemade sauce. Make sure you don't burn your tomatoes, Joey!

    Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Organic Lasagna
  • Italian Seasonins

    Steps

  1. Cook tomatoes
  2. Puree tomatoes
  3. Boil Pasta
  4. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese twice
  5. Bake for 30 min at 375 degrees
